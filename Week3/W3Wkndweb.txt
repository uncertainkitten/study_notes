What is a Load Balancer?
A load balancer is a device or program intended to distribute requests to a distributed a system

What are some examples of Horizontal Scaling?
Really anything that involves adding more machines to your system:  server farms and the like

What is Moore's Law?
The number of transistors in a dense integrated circuit doubles roughly every 2 years.
